## Mandate
To ensure that the technical infrastructure of Web3Privacy Now is stable, secure, and accessible.

## Responsibilities
* Manage and maintain all servers and hosting environments.
* Administer our shared communication platforms (e.g., Telegram, Matrix).
* Maintain a public **Service Catalog** of all tools and platforms we run.
* Implement and manage user access policies and permissions.
* Monitor for security vulnerabilities and ensure services are reliable.

## How to Engage
**For support requests** or to report an issue, or **to join the Guild** and contribute please visit the [#infra](https://commons.w3pn.org/#narrow/channel/23-infra) channel.
